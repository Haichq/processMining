Alpha Miner Introduction

 The α-algorithm or α-miner is an algorithm used in process mining, aimed at reconstructing causality from a set of sequences of events.

 The goal of Alpha miner is to convert the event log into a workflow-net based on the relations between various activities in the event log.

 An event log is a multi-set of traces, and a trace is a sequence of activity names.
